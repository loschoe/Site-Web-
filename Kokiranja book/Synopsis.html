In the shadowy realm of Raventide, a city strangled by the merciless grip of the notorious Hell's Circle, the unsuspecting Razy, daughter of the feared terrorist leader, finds her world colliding with the son of her father's enemy, Leant. Despite the volatile tapestry of their families' rivalries, an unyielding attraction blossoms between them, forcing Razy and Leant to navigate a treacherous dance of love and loyalty as they grapple with the stark reality that their hearts' desires could lead to their ultimate downfall or the city's redemption.
